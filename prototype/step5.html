STEP 5. Complete Paperwork

1-Residency

To qualify for New York City tuition rates, students must:

  • Have completed one year of residency in New York City prior to the first day of classes
  • Not be on a temporary visa
  • Be a U.S. citizen, permanent resident, refugee/Asylum granted (I-94 card) or in a non-immigrant status that qualifies for the resident rate.

2-Immunization

Prior to registration, you must show proof that you have been immunized.

No student will be allowed to register until all immunization records are complete.


To Complete Immunization Paperwork

  • Please download and print the Immunization-Clearance form
  • have it completed by your healthcare provider
  • return it to the Health Services Office in Room N380

Did You Know?:

N.Y.S. Health Law #2165 requires proof of immunization against measles, mumps, and rubella (MMR) in order to attend school if you were born after 1956.

Free immunizations for measles, mumps and rubella (MMR) are provided by the Student Health Services Office (N380) throughout the year. Please contact the office at 221-220-8255 for dates and times.

Meningitis vaccines are not offered.

Questions?

If you have any questions you can call the Student Health Services Office at 212-220-8256 or visit in person in room N380 Monday – Friday 8 am to 5 pm.

3-Commitment deposit

After you accept BMCC’s offer you are required to pay a $100 non-refundable commitment deposit. This deposit reserves your space in the entering class.

The $100 will count as a credit towards your tuition and fee charges. If you later decide not to attend BMCC you will not receive a refund on the money.

Waivers

You may qualify to have the deposit waived so you don’t have to pay it. To qualify you must meet ONE of the following options:

  • You have filed a 2014-15 Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and your Student Aid Report (SAR) has an Expected Family Contribution (EFC) of 3000 or less.
  • You have been accepted to the College Discovery Program (Subject to verification by the College Discovery Office).
  • You are a Veteran.

Commitment Deposit Form

Whether you are paying the deposit or requesting a waiver you have to fill out the same form. Please print out the commitment deposit form and follow the directions. Submit this form with payment (check or money order only) by mail or in person to the Bursar's office at

Bursar’s Office room S330 199 Chambers Street New York, NY 10007 Click here to download the Commitment Deposit Form (PDF). Deadline The commitment deposit or waiver request is due within 15 days of receiving your acceptance message.
