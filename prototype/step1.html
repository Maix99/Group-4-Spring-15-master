Step 1: Accept The Offer

Welcome to BMCC!

Are you ready to begin your successful academic journey with us? If so START HERE and complete this form. Completing the I Accept form is the formal way of telling BMCC that you want to be a student at the college and will register for classes. It is an important and necessary first step to being a BMCC student.
