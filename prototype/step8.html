Step 8: Register for class

After you have gone through orientation and have been advised, you will be ready to register for classes. First, you need to login to cunyfirst.

Login to CUNYfirst

Once you log in, follow the steps below to begin adding classes to your shopping cart.


1.Click HR/Campus Solutions -> Self Service -> Class Search/Browse Catalog ->

2.Under Search Criteria, select at least 2 search fields (subject, course number). To further refine your search, select Additional Search Criteria. click search


Review the search results and choose the sections that best fit your schedule by clicking .

Review the class details and select Next. The class has now been added to your Shopping Cart.


Click and then *Your registration is not complete until you click Finish Enrolling*

If successful, you will see the status next to the class. If there is an error, you will see the status next to the class.


Continue Steps 4-8 until you have enrolled in all of your classes.
