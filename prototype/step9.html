Step 9: Bursar

You must first register for class before you get your bill.

Paying your bill

You may finalize your bill in one or more of the following ways:

  • Paying tuition and fees in full with Financial Aid
    If your financial aid grants and loans appear on your account as pending aid or anticipated aid, no further action with the Bursar is required; your bill is automatically considered finalized.
  • Pay tuition and fees online with credit card or electronic check transfer.
    Tuition plans are available fall and spring only.
  • By a Third Party Voucher from your employer.
    Obtain a voucher from your employer and present it at the time of registration to defer your tuition. Third party vouchers are applicable only when the college is authorized to directly bill the company. Employee reimbursement plans are not considered third party.
  • By obtaining a Veteran's Deferral.
    In order to ensure that class reservations for the semester, try to complete your registration and bill payment as soon as you can but no later than close of business on assigned date
