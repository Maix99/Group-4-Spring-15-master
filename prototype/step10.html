Step 10: How Do I Obtain my BMCC I.D. Card?

The Seven steps for the BMCC ID Card

  1. BMCC ID cards are issued at the very end of the entire registration process—after you’ve chosen your classes, and after your bill has been finalized.
  2. IDs are created and distributed, in a specified room each semester.
  3. After the registration period, new IDs are created in their permanent ID Office room, in S-215 of the Main Building.
  4. Your ID card gives you access to the college and all its activities, including, labs and library, as well as access to all CUNY campuses and their libraries.
  5. BMCC has agreements with many organizations (i.e. museums) whom offer discounted or free admission with your BMCC I.D. Card.
  6. Also, many businesses in the area of the school offer student discounts on meals and other purchases.
  7. Search within the BMCC website to see which museums and fun extras your ID card can take you.
