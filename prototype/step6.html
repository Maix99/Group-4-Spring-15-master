Step : New Student Orientation

All new students are required to attend new student orientation before beginning the advisement and registration process. There are two opportunities to attend new student orientation: Getting Prepared to Start (GPS) or In-Person Registration Orientation. Getting Prepared to Start (GPS) orientation is offered between May and mid-August. Through GPS, students are able to connect with current students serving as GPS Navigators (orientation leaders) as well as staff and take advantage of early registration. GPS is not only a way for incoming students to become oriented to start their first year, but also a way to connect with a growing community of new students through social media. To obtain a GPS appointment and attend GPS orientation, please click on the following link: http://www.bmcc.cuny.edu/gpsfaq/

In-Person Registration

In-person Registration (IPR) Orientation occurs from the start of In-Person Registration until the start of classes. The IPR orientation is designed to orient incoming students to the college/registration process in approximately 30 minutes.
